My Way Of Working.

Step One / We Talk

Fill up the contact form and then we schedule a 1:1 strategy call. We take an in-depth look at your business goals, your audience, and what are your needs. After you answered all my questions and me yours, I will start the design process. This will make sure we’re on the same page before we start creating your projetc.

Step Two / Create

Once we agreed on price, design ...we can start ! Final moodboard, wireframing, branding etc... At this stage, you’ll be able to share constructive feedback and we’ll work on any refinements as needed.

Step Three / Review

We will refine concepts based on your feedback. Once approved, we create what you have chosen for your project. Lastly, we put everything together and check it out together.

Step Four / Delivery

Last but not least, we carefully organize all your files and send over a link to download your final brand assets. If it's a Website, you'll get all you need to learn how to use our CMS.
